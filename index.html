Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about leasing with Maison Liann Estates.

What are your standard lease terms?

For residential properties, we typically require a minimum lease term of one (1) year. For commercial spaces, terms usually range from three (3) to five (5) years, depending on the scale and location of the property.

What documents are required to secure a lease?

Standard requirements include valid government-issued IDs, proof of income (such as Certificate of Employment or latest ITR), and post-dated checks (PDCs) for the monthly rent. Corporate lessees will need to provide SEC registration and a Secretary's Certificate.

Do you offer property management services for landlords?

Yes. Maison Liann Estates offers end-to-end property management. We handle tenant sourcing, rigorous screening, lease contract administration, rent collection, and maintenance coordination.

Can foreigners lease properties in the Philippines?

Absolutely. Foreigners are legally allowed to lease residential and commercial properties. You will simply need to provide a copy of your passport and Alien Certificate of Registration (ACR) if applicable.
